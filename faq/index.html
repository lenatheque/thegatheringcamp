Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Gathering Camp retreat?

The Gathering camp will take place in the mountains of Vancouver, all members will be meet in our office located at 4080 Robson St, Vancouver, BC V6B 3K9 in the day of the event.

What kind of team is right for the Gathering Camp retreat?

The Gathering Camp is an intimate 4-day camp in the mountains of Vancouver, Canada for millennial girl bosses, freelancers, solopreneurs and CEOs to connect, reset and recharge.

What size teams can you accommodate?

The Gathering Camp accommodates for 35 girl bosses each session. Our services include everything from boutique accommodations, healthy meals, camp activities, swag bags and more!

Can I change or cancel my reservation after it is placed

No, once an reservation has been placed it enters a very automated system and no changes can be made to the contents of the order.
